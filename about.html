★ About us

Get to know us

A team of experienced professionals

Founded in 2022, Lofi Studio started with a dedicated focus on creating album and EP covers for Lofi Records. Our journey soon took a creative turn, leading us to design animations for the notable Lofi Girl compilation. As our team grew, so did our vision, prompting us to expand the studio.

Today, Lofi Studio is a vibrant mix of illustrators, animators, and designers from around the world. We all share a deep passion for art and storytelling. Beyond our collaborations with big names like Disney and Psyonix, we pride ourselves on our versatility across various styles. Under the watchful eye of our art director, we ensure that every client collaborates with a team that’s dedicated and trustworthy.
